Oman Collegiate Programming Contest


What is this registration form for?
This form is meant for the team's coaches who want to participate in The 10th Oman Collegiate Programming Contest (OCPC2023) and The 11th Gulf Programming Contest (GPC2023). Both events will be held in Muscat during the period 28/Oct - 4/Nov/2023. Omani teams can opt to participate in one or both contests; GCC teams can only register in GPC2023. The accepted teams in any of the contests will be notified by 17/Oct/2023.


General Instructions:

  1. Make all needed documents ready before start filling the form.
  2. This form should be filled by one person from the team; preferably the team's coach only.
  3. Fill the names, DoB and all other information as they appear in the official documents. If you have more than one team, you need to fill separate form for each team and repeat the details of the coach (in case a coach is having multiple teams) within each form.
  4. Filling the pre-registration form is not a guarantee for the acceptance. All accepted teams will be notified by email after auditing the documents.
  5. IMPORTANT NOTICE 1: for GCC teams, if any of your team-members require visa to enter Oman then we encourage you to apply ahead by enough time to the concerned authorities (e.g. Oman embassy at your residence country); We will not be able to provide any support on this matter due the workload on the organization team.
  6. IMPORTANT NOTICE 2: for team from outside Oman/GCC countries, if any of your teams-members require VISA assistant to enter Oman then the team's registration must be confirmed before 10/Oct/2023; and must notify us by email before the specified date. Visa processing fees might be applied, and the visa-issuing is not guaranteed.


Deadlines and Notifications:
1. The deadline to pre-register in GPC2024/OCPC2024 using this form is 20/Oct/2023.
2. The acceptance notification will be sent by email on 12/Oct/2023.


Age Exemption Procedure
If any of your team contestants is not satisfying the participation age restriction, then please send a request for exemption to the organization team via this email: events@codeacademy.om. The request will be judged by the GPC/OCPC steering committee and you will be notified about the outcomes. Please do not register the team/member until you got notified.


Registration Fee Payment:
To make it easy for the teams, the registration fees can be paid at the site during the first day of the event. The payment can be paid cash only, and an official receipt will be provided if needed.


Documents (Get them ready-scanned before start filling the form):
1. Coach's ID + photo
2. Coach's employment card (or coach-ship authorization letter from the institute)
3. First contestant's id + enrolment letter + photo
4. Second contestant's id + enrolment letter + photo
5. Third contestant's id + enrolment letter + photo


Inquiries:
For more information and inquiries please send us an email via events@codeacademy.om. Or you can call the team on 00968 97451040. Also you can visit https://bit.ly/3r1hYVL for more information about the contests and how to prepare for them.
